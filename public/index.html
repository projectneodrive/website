Rethinking Personal Mobility

Project Neodrive is a research initiative exploring light, weather-protected, human-scaled vehicles that combine what people value in cars with the social and climate efficiency of bicycles.

Our mission: Drastically reduce the impact of personal mobility on the world.

Purpose of this website

This site is organized as a series of interconnected chapters. Start from the beginning or jump to what interests you. The goal is to present our research direction and findings.
